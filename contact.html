Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register as a new student?

To register as a new student, visit our Student Registration page and fill out the complete form with your personal, academic, and contact information. After submission, you'll receive a confirmation email with your login credentials.

How can I check my attendance and marks?

After logging in to your student account, you can access your attendance records and marks from the dashboard. Navigate to the "Attendance" or "My Marks" sections to view detailed reports of your academic progress.

What should I do if I forget my login password?

If you forget your password, contact the IT Support department at support@edumanage.edu or call (555) 123-4573. They will help you reset your password and regain access to your account.

How can parents access their child's academic information?

Parents can request access to their child's academic information by contacting the Student Services department. We provide regular progress reports and can arrange parent-teacher meetings to discuss academic performance.

What are the system requirements for accessing the portal?

The EduManage system works on any modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) with internet connectivity. It's responsive and works on desktop computers, tablets, and mobile devices. No additional software installation is required.
